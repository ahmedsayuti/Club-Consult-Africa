Who We Are

Club Consult Africa is the continent's premier football agency, specializing in professional player representation, career development, and strategic partnerships.

Founded with a vision to bridge African football talent with global opportunities, we have established ourselves as trusted partners for professional footballers across the continent. Our deep understanding of African football culture combined with international expertise sets us apart in the industry.

Our Services

Comprehensive career management services designed to maximize player potential, career longevity, and financial success.

Athlete Representation

We provide dedicated, personalized management with 24/7 support. Our team handles all aspects of your professional career including contract management, career planning, and daily support services.

Contract Negotiations

Our experienced negotiators ensure maximum value in every contract. We specialize in transfer agreements, salary negotiations, performance bonuses, and image rights management.

Endorsements & Sponsorships

We build and manage personal brands through strategic partnerships with global brands. Our marketing team develops customized brand strategies for each athlete.

Career Development

We provide career planning, skills development, and educational support to ensure longevity in professional football and successful transition to post-career opportunities.

Scouting & Recruitment

Our extensive scouting network across Africa identifies promising talent. We provide comprehensive evaluation and development pathways for young athletes.

Wellness & Support

We provide access to sports psychologists, nutritionists, and medical professionals to ensure our athletes maintain peak physical and mental condition.
