CCA Elite Scouting Tournament 2026 Announced

Global scouts and FIFA officials to converge in Ghana for landmark youth football event showcasing over 120 talents aged 16-21 from January 5-12, 2026.

January 1, 2026 8 min read By CCA Press Office
Back to All Articles

UNION PARK, University of Ghana - Venue for the CCA Elite Scouting Tournament 2026

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Global Attention for Youth Football Talent

Club Consult Africa Limited has unveiled the CCA Elite Scouting Tournament 2026, set to take place at UNION PARK, University of Ghana from January 5–7 and January 9–12, 2026. The event will showcase over 120 young football talents aged 16–21 from eight youth teams, with renowned global scouts and FIFA officials in attendance.

"This tournament represents a landmark moment for African youth football. We're not just discovering talent; we're creating pathways for these young athletes to reach their full potential on the global stage."

Tournament Details

The tournament will feature 8 youth teams from Ghana, showcasing over 120 promising young talents between the ages of 16 and 21. What makes this event truly special is the presence of renowned global scouts and FIFA officials, who will be in attendance to observe and engage with the next generation of African football stars.

Club Consult Africa is calling on all media houses in print, broadcast, and digital to partner with us in giving this landmark youth football event the visibility it deserves. Coverage will not only highlight the talent on display but also underscore Ghana's growing reputation as a hub for youth football development with international recognition.

Young talents will have the opportunity to showcase their skills to international scouts

Key Reasons to Cover the Tournament

  • Global Attention: Renowned scouts and FIFA officials will be present, offering media exclusive access to international football decision-makers.
  • Talent Discovery: A rare opportunity to witness future stars at the grassroots level before they emerge on the global stage.
  • Football Development: Reinforcing Ghana's reputation as a breeding ground for world-class players and a model for youth development in Africa.
  • Community Engagement: Inspiring young athletes and promoting sports culture across communities in Ghana and beyond.

Media Partnership Opportunities

Media houses are invited to partner with Club Consult Africa in covering this landmark event. Partnership benefits include:

  • Exclusive access to player interviews and behind-the-scenes content
  • Press conferences with scouts and FIFA officials
  • Branding opportunities at the tournament venue
  • Access to high-quality footage and photography
  • Interview opportunities with Club Consult Africa executives

We would be honored by your presence at this event and look forward to welcoming you in Accra.
