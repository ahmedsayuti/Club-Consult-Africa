Samuel Owusu Signs for Saudi Giants Al-Ahli Jeddah

Ghanaian winger Samuel Owusu completes season-long loan move to Al-Ahli Jeddah from Al Fayha SC, presented with his cherished number 19 shirt at official unveiling.

February 10, 2026 8 min read By Kwame Asante
Back to All Articles

Samuel Owusu unveiled as Al-Ahli Jeddah's new signing with his signature number 19 shirt

Transfer Completed: Owusu Joins Al-Ahli Jeddah

Samuel Owusu has been unveiled by his new Saudi Arabian club Al-Ahli Jeddah. The Ghanaian winger signed for the Black and Whites on a season-long loan contract from Al Fayha SC over the weekend, and on Monday, Owusu was presented to the media and club's fans for the first time.

"This is a proud moment for me and my family. Al-Ahli Jeddah is a club with great tradition and ambition, and I'm excited to contribute to their success. Wearing the number 19 shirt here means a lot to me - it's been with me through my journey."

- Samuel Owusu

The Iconic Number 19

At his unveiling on Monday, Owusu was handed his dearest number 19 shirt which he has become accustomed to in recent times. He wore the number at Serbian side FK Čukarički, Al Fayha and the Ghana national team, making it a significant part of his football identity.

The number 19 has special significance for Owusu, representing continuity and success throughout his career progression from Ghana to Europe and now in Saudi Arabia.

19 Samuel Owusu - Player Profile

Position: Right Winger
Age: 27 years
Nationality: Ghanaian
Previous Club: Al Fayha SC (Saudi Arabia)
Contract Type: Season-long loan
Ghana Caps: 7 appearances
Int'l Debut Goal: vs Qatar (October 2025)
Major Tournament: 2019 Africa Cup of Nations

Career Journey: From Vision FC to International Stardom

Samuel Owusu's football journey is a testament to consistent development and seizing opportunities. His career progression shows a steady climb through different levels of competition:

  • Vision FC (Ghana): Started his professional career at the Ghanaian club, developing his technical skills and earning attention with his dribbling ability
  • FK Čukarički (Serbia): First European move where he adapted to European football, wearing the number 19 shirt and establishing himself as a key player
  • Al Fayha SC (Saudi Arabia): Moved to the Saudi Professional League, continuing with number 19 and adapting to Middle Eastern football
  • Ghana National Team: Earned his debut in 2018, was part of the 2019 AFCON squad, and recently scored his first international goal against Qatar
  • Al-Ahli Jeddah: New challenge at one of Saudi Arabia's most prestigious clubs, maintaining his number 19 tradition

Samuel Owusu poses with Al-Ahli Jeddah officials during his official presentation

Recent International Success

Owusu recently netted his debut goal for the Black Stars during their friendly match against Qatar earlier this month. This milestone came in his 7th appearance for Ghana and has boosted his confidence heading into this new club chapter.

"Scoring for Ghana was a dream come true," Owusu said. "It gave me extra motivation and confidence. I want to bring that scoring form to Al-Ahli Jeddah and help the team achieve its objectives."

What Owusu Brings to Al-Ahli Jeddah

Club officials have identified several key attributes that make Owusu a valuable addition to their squad:

Technical Qualities

  • Exceptional dribbling skills in one-on-one situations
  • Accurate crossing ability from wide positions
  • Improving goal-scoring record from midfield positions
  • Good vision and passing range in the final third

Tactical Adaptability

  • Can play on either wing or as an attacking midfielder
  • Experience in different tactical systems across multiple leagues
  • Understanding of defensive responsibilities in modern systems
  • Ability to play in high-tempo or possession-based approaches

International Experience

  • 7 caps for the Ghana national team with recent goal-scoring form
  • Participation in the 2019 Africa Cup of Nations
  • Experience in high-pressure international matches
  • Adaptability to different football cultures and environments

Club Consult Africa's Role in the Transfer

As Samuel Owusu's official representatives, Club Consult Africa played a crucial role in facilitating this strategic loan move to Al-Ahli Jeddah. Our team worked closely with both clubs to ensure a smooth transition for the player.

"Samuel's move to Al-Ahli Jeddah represents an important step in his career development. His consistent performances in Saudi Arabia and recent success with the national team made him an attractive option for a top club like Al-Ahli. We're confident he'll excel in this new environment."

The agency's comprehensive support included:

  • Negotiation of favorable loan terms with Al-Ahli Jeddah
  • Coordination between Al Fayha SC and Al-Ahli Jeddah
  • Ensuring the player's number 19 shirt preference was honored
  • Support for the player's transition to a new club environment
  • Ongoing career management and development planning

Al-Ahli Jeddah's Ambitions

Al-Ahli Jeddah, known as "The Black and Whites," is one of Saudi Arabia's most successful and popular clubs. The club has shown significant ambition in recent transfer windows, aiming to strengthen their squad with quality international talent.

Owusu's arrival aligns with the club's strategy of blending experienced international players with local talent to compete for domestic honors and make an impact in Asian competitions.

Future Prospects and International Outlook

At 27, Samuel Owusu is entering what should be the peak years of his career. His loan move to Al-Ahli Jeddah represents an opportunity to establish himself at a top Saudi club and potentially earn a permanent transfer based on his performances.

With consistent performances at club level and his recent breakthrough goal for Ghana, Owusu will be aiming to secure a more regular place in the Black Stars squad for upcoming competitions, including World Cup qualifiers and the next Africa Cup of Nations.

Conclusion: Samuel Owusu's loan move to Al-Ahli Jeddah represents a significant career progression for the Ghanaian winger. His familiarity with Saudi Arabian football, combined with his recent international success and cherished number 19 shirt, positions him well for success at his new club. Club Consult Africa is proud to have facilitated this important move and looks forward to supporting Samuel's continued development as he aims to make an impact at one of Saudi Arabia's most prestigious clubs.
