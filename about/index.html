Our Journey Since 2012

Redefining Athlete Representation in Africa

We are more than just a sports management agency. We are strategic partners dedicated to unlocking the full potential of African athletes through innovative management, personalized representation, and global opportunities.

2012

Our Founding Vision

Club Consult Africa was founded in 2012 on a revolutionary belief: that more is required of an athlete representative than simply providing assistance in negotiating contracts. We recognized that African talent deserved more comprehensive support to truly excel on the global stage.

Our founders—former professional footballers, experienced attorneys, and marketing experts—came together with a shared vision: to create an agency that would challenge conventional approaches to sports management and provide holistic career development for African athletes.

From our humble beginnings in Accra, Ghana, we have grown into a pan-African powerhouse with presence across three continents. Yet, our core philosophy remains unchanged: to deliver exceptional value through deep, meaningful relationships with our clients.

"We continue to provide unique and innovative management to sports professionals by fostering relationships with our clients and challenging ourselves to continually bring innovative ways of thinking to the most complex challenges we may face together."

Our Core Beliefs

The principles that guide every decision we make for our clients

Transcending Athletic Excellence

We believe there is a great opportunity for athletes to transcend their popularity, visibility, and resources into areas far greater than athletic endeavors. Our role is to help athletes build legacies that extend beyond the field.

Maximizing Unique Potential

We challenge each client to recognize their unique position and maximize long-term possibilities. Every athlete has distinct strengths, and our strategies are tailored to amplify what makes them exceptional.

Relationships First

The key to long-term successful career management begins with establishing close, personal relationships between our staff and our clients and their families. We become part of your team, not just your representatives.

Our Leadership

Meet the visionary behind Club Consult Africa

CEO & Founder

Yussif Alhassan Chibsah

Chief Executive Officer & Founder

Former Professional Ghanaian & International Footballer

Yussif Alhassan Chibsah is a distinguished former professional footballer who represented Ghana at the highest international level. With over a decade of experience playing professionally in Ghana and internationally, he brings an insider's understanding of the football industry that few can match.

After hanging up his boots, Yussif channeled his passion for the game into creating Club Consult Africa in 2012. His vision was simple yet revolutionary: to create an agency that understood athletes from the inside out, combining professional expertise with genuine empathy for the athlete's journey.

Under his leadership, Club Consult Africa has grown from a local startup to an internationally recognized agency with presence across three continents. Yussif's hands-on approach and deep industry connections ensure that every client receives personalized attention and access to opportunities that align with their unique career aspirations.

Our Evolution Timeline

Key milestones in our journey of excellence and innovation

The Foundation

Club Consult Africa was founded in Accra, Ghana, with a vision to revolutionize athlete representation. Our first office opened with a dedicated team of three professionals committed to changing the game.

2012

First Major Breakthrough

We facilitated our first international transfer, moving a promising Ghanaian talent to a European club. This milestone validated our approach and expanded our network beyond Africa's borders.

2014

European Expansion

Opened our first European office in Lisbon, Portugal, establishing a physical presence to better serve our growing roster of clients competing in top European leagues.

2017

Strategic Global Partnerships

Forged key partnerships with MLS clubs and established connections with major sports networks and brands in North America, expanding our reach across the Atlantic.

2020

Industry Recognition

Reached the significant milestone of representing over 50 professional footballers across Africa, Europe, and North America, solidifying our position as a leading agency.

2023

Elite Scouting Tournament

Announced our inaugural Elite Scouting Tournament, bringing together over 120 young African talents with global scouts and FIFA officials to showcase the future of African football.
